October for iOS

Privacy Policy

What information do we collect

October for iOS does not store or transmit personal information. The October app does not include or use any analytics or advertising software. We do not operate any servers that collect data.

Cloud documents

The user is able to save Song documents to cloud services integrated with the iOS Files app, such as iCloud, DropBox, OneDrive, etc. Usage of these cloud storage services is completely optional, and the user also has the option to save documents locally to the device. We do not have access to any October Song documents saved locally or to the cloud.
